Vince's Soul Care Movies

My Top Three

The Tree of Life

A coming of age movie that explores human value, loss, and manhood.

Silence

Where is God? Is He good? Can I endure? This film unpacks these questions (and more)!

Fight Club

I'm not supposed to talk about it, but Tyler Durden wrestles with whether or not to embrace reality, or to choose an idiosyncratic concept of meaning.
